Research Areas

We apply systems biology and machine learning approaches to study complex biological systems

Transcriptional Regulation

Understanding transcriptional regulation of bacterial pathogens including Salmonella and E. coli using ChIP-seq and RNA-seq analysis

High-Value Bacterial Strains

Characterization and optimization of industrially important bacterial strains for biotechnology applications

Anti-Microbial Resistance

Investigating mechanisms of antimicrobial resistance and developing strategies to combat resistant pathogens

Machine Learning

Developing ML frameworks for biological data analysis, battery health management, and energy systems

Genome-Scale Modeling

Reconstructing and analyzing genome-scale metabolic models for understanding complex biological systems

Energy Systems

Machine learning-based frameworks for battery prognostics and hydrogen production feasibility prediction
